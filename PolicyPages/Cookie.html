Cookie Policy

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience, understand how our website is used, and ensure smooth and secure functionality.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your actions and preferences.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep items in your shopping cart
  • Remember your login and preferences
  • Understand how visitors use our website
  • Improve website performance and user experience

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality (cart, checkout, login)
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand visitor behavior to improve the site
  • Marketing Cookies: May be used to show relevant offers and promotions

4. Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. However, some parts of the website (like checkout) may not function properly without cookies.
