WELCOME TO GAJENDRA VILAS

Welcome to Gajendra Vilas, where every sweet, savoury, and bakery delight carries the warmth of tradition and the joy of sharing. Our journey began in 1978 with a simple dream — to preserve our family’s cherished recipes and bring authentic flavors to every home. What started as a small step rooted in heritage has grown into a name loved and trusted by multiple generations.

We believe food is more than just something you eat — it is comfort, celebration, and connection. From festive occasions and family gatherings to everyday cravings and thoughtful gifts, our creations are made to be part of life’s special moments. Each product is carefully prepared using quality ingredients, time-honored methods, and a commitment to freshness.
